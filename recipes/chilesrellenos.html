Chiles Rellenos

Delicious stuffed chiles rellenos with cheese and red sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 large poblano peppers
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Roast the poblano peppers over an open flame or under a broiler until the skin is charred. Place them in a plastic bag to sweat for about 10 minutes, then peel off the skin. Make a slit down the side of each pepper and remove the seeds.
  2. Stuff each pepper with shredded Monterey Jack cheese.
  3. In a bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form. In another bowl, beat the egg yolks until thickened. Gently fold the yolks into the whites.
  4. Dredge each stuffed pepper in flour, then dip it into the egg mixture, ensuring it is well coated.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry each pepper until golden brown on all sides. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. In a separate pan, saut√© chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  7. Pour the tomato sauce over the fried chiles rellenos and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious chiles rellenos!
