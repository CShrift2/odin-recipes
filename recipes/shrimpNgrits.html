Shrimp n Grits

A plate of delicious shrimp n grits

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup stone-ground grits
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil. Slowly whisk in the grits. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  2. While the grits are cooking, in a large skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove bacon with a slotted spoon and set aside, leaving the bacon fat in the skillet.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the shrimp to the skillet. Season with paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side until shrimp are pink and opaque. Remove shrimp from skillet and set aside.
  5. Once the grits are cooked, stir in the shredded cheddar cheese and heavy cream. Mix until cheese is melted and grits are creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. To serve, spoon the cheesy grits onto plates, top with cooked shrimp, crispy bacon, and chopped green onions.
  7. Enjoy your delicious Shrimp n Grits!
